Two Bedroom Suite:

The 812 sq. ft unrenovated Two Bedroom Suite features a king bed in the master bedroom, a double bed and a twin bed in the second bedroom, one private bathroom with shower/tub combination, a separate living area, fully equipped kitchen plus a breakfast nook with dining table for four, and television with premium channels and DVD player. All Two Bedroom Suites feature the original decor and room setup and are not renovated. These suites can be found in our oceanfront building near the Ocean Deck (please note that these suites do not offer an ocean view). The Ocean Deck lies directly in front of the East end of the building and offers quick and convenient direct beach access.
